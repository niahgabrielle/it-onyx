Booking Policy

We require you to put a credit card on file.

Why do we require a credit card for booking?

If a guest refuses to provide us with card information it usually indicates that they are unsure they are going to make the appointment and will potentially cancel last minute, or worse no-show.

Cancelation Policy

We know life happens and everything isn’t predictable, but we kindly ask that any changes and cancellations be made 24 hours prior to your scheduled appointment. Please note that appointment times are reserved especially for you. All clients will be required to keep a credit card on file. Failure to call and reschedule 24 hours before your appointment is subject to 50% or full cost of the scheduled appointment at the salon’s discretion. Out of respect for our guests, services for clients arriving more than fifteen minutes late will be honored at your stylist’s discretion. We reserve the right to cancel or modify your services as needed to stay on time for all following appointments. Thank you!
